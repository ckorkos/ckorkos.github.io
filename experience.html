Skinny Gene Project

Design and Marketing Intern: August-December 2016

Responsibilities:

  • Graphic and Logo Design
  • Redesign for Company Site and Documents
  • Writing and Recording Interviews with Users and Partners
  • Coordination with Marketing Team to plan Company Events
  • Creating Presentations for Marketing Head

Skinny Gene Project is a non-profit which works towards the prevention of prediabetes in predominantly low income communities. Their services include classes that teach healthier lifestyles as well as promotion of fitness centers and useful diets to reduce sugar consumption.

At this position, I worked alongside a team of interns specializing in business development, social media, graphic design, and marketing to help create a brand for the non-profit. Specifically, our main task as a group was to advertise SGP's presence at a health awareness-focused 5K run. While I entered with experience in design and programming, I was fortunate to spend more time working with these in a professional environment. I also learned skills in social media writing, event planning and advertising, and general business marketing. This was a side of the business world I was previously unfamiliar with, and time spent in this environment made me a more business-minded worker.

Samples of my work at Skinny Gene Project can be viewed under the Projects tab.

User Interface Designer: June-August 2016

Club Assistant, LLC

Project Manager for Design Consulting: April-June 2016

Responsibilities:

  • Communicating with Client
  • Organizing Team and Client Meetings
  • Creating PowerPoint Presentations
  • Providing Quality Assurance for Submitted Work

This design consultation was for course credit in Computer Science 199: Independent Study. I managed a team of designers and developers to create an original design for Club Assistant, LLC under the guidance and mentorship of Professor Mary Boyle. Our goal was to create a design that fixed visual and interaction issues with the existing site.

My previous work had been almost exclusively focused on design and user research, with some time in web development. This project taught me the management side of design, and gave me important experience in the organizational tools used across most professional teams.

Visual Design and Development: January-March 2016

Responsibilities:
